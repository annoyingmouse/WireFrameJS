Note

If using a grid layout, and why wouldn't you, please ensure the images are 10% larger than the grid elements, and that the elements are square.

The following is interactive; click on a square and it will rotate through the possible combinations of the wc-kolam, or remove it altogether. You can change the colour of the lines and dots.

You can print the result to a canvas element (each print is added at the bottom of the page), so you can save it to your local machine as a rastered image.
